7 RnB Artists You Should Be Listening To

Read Time:7 minutes

The world of R&B has always been a sanctuary for soulful melodies, rich storytelling, and emotive expression. From its golden eras of the '90s to today, the genre continues to evolve, with new and seasoned voices emerging to captivate fans. If you're a lover of R&B, these seven artists are absolutely essential for your playlist. With their recent releases, they’ve not only defined modern R&B but have also added their own unique touch to the sound. Here's why you should be listening to them right now.

1. Kehlani

Recent Releases: Crash & While We Wait 2

Release Date: 2024

Kehlani has solidified herself as a staple in contemporary R&B with her raw vulnerability and smooth vocals. With her latest album Crash, released earlier in 2024, Kehlani offered a blend of emotional honesty and upbeat production that resonated with both long-time fans and new listeners. Crash explores themes of love, self-discovery, and healing—everything that makes Kehlani such a key artist for anyone who cherishes emotionally driven R&B.

Her follow-up ep in August 2024, While We Wait 2, a sequel to her popular 2019 mixtape, has only further proven her consistency. Fans and critics alike praised the album for its mature sound, reflecting the personal growth she’s experienced since her earlier days. The project sparked conversations about self-love and emotional independence, making it a favorite among listeners.

2. Cat Burns

Recent Releases: Early Twenties

Release Date: 2024

Cat Burns, a rising star in the R&B scene, is known for her soulful voice and deeply personal lyrics. Her debut album Early Twenties, released in 2024, is an introspective journey into the emotional highs and lows of growing up and finding one's identity. It perfectly captures the essence of modern-day coming-of-age experiences, touching on love, heartbreak, and mental health.

Early Twenties was met with rave reviews from both critics and fans alike. R&B listeners, in particular, appreciated her honest approach to songwriting, and her ability to express emotions many young adults are currently navigating. As a newcomer, Cat Burns brings a fresh voice to the genre while staying true to its introspective roots.

My Personal Favourite

End Game

3. Lucky Daye

Recent Releases: Algorithm

Release Date: 2024

Lucky Daye continues to redefine what R&B can sound like, and his 2024 album Algorithm is the perfect example of his innovation. Known for blending old-school R&B vibes with futuristic production, Lucky Daye effortlessly stands out in the crowded music scene. His smooth falsetto, combined with intricate melodies and beats, has garnered him a growing fan base and industry respect.

Algorithm was a hit among fans, who praised its creative take on love, relationships, and personal growth. R&B purists celebrated his homage to the genre’s history, while younger audiences loved his modern twist. The album’s release cemented Lucky Daye as a key artist to watch as R&B continues to evolve.

My Personal Favourite

HERicane

4. Jazmine Sullivan

Recent Releases: Heaux Tales

Release Date: 2021

Though released in 2021, Jazmine Sullivan's Heaux Tales continues to be one of the most talked-about R&B projects. The album’s narrative-driven approach explores themes of femininity, love, and empowerment, offering listeners a bold commentary on societal expectations placed on women. Each track is a tale—an introspective look into the lives of women navigating love, sex, and relationships.

Heaux Tales received widespread acclaim from critics and fans alike, praised for its boldness and emotional depth. Sullivan's powerful voice and ability to deliver raw emotion in her music have made this project an instant classic. It’s essential listening for any R&B fan seeking substance and storytelling in their music.

My Personal Favourite

5. Victoria Monét

Recent Releases: Jaguar II

Release Date: 2023

Victoria Monét’s Jaguar II is a celebration of sensuality, confidence, and empowerment. Released in 2023, this album marked her as a standout figure in the R&B world, blending elements of funk, soul, and R&B into a cohesive body of work. Victoria’s songwriting is sharp, witty, and sultry, with songs that embrace both personal and sexual freedom.

Jaguar was praised for its unique production and Monét’s ability to command attention with her voice. R&B fans were quick to rally around this release, celebrating it for its blend of 70s funk grooves and modern R&B flair. With each release, Victoria Monét proves she’s an artist worth paying attention to for anyone who values artistry and evolution in R&B.

My Personal Favourite

6. Tems

Recent Releases: Born in The Wild

Release Date: 2024

Nigerian artist Tems has become a global sensation, bringing her unique fusion of Afrobeats and R&B to a worldwide audience. Her 2023 release Born in The Wild is a breathtaking exploration of freedom, resilience, and self-awareness. Tems’ smoky vocals and ethereal production have enchanted listeners, making her one of the most exciting and important voices in modern R&B.

Born in The Wild received glowing praise for its bold fusion of genres and for Tems’ ability to weave deeply emotional narratives. R&B fans, in particular, have appreciated her soulful delivery and unique sound, which manages to feel both familiar and entirely new. If you haven’t been listening to Tems, now’s the time.

My Personal Favourite

Love Me JeJe

7. Leon Thomas

Recent Releases: Electric Dusk

Release Date: 2023

Leon Thomas has been quietly working behind the scenes as a songwriter and producer for some of the biggest names in music, but his 2023 album Electric Dusk puts him firmly in the spotlight. The project showcases his immense talent not only as a vocalist but as a multi-instrumentalist and producer. Blending R&B, jazz, and funk, Electric Dusk is an album that takes risks while remaining deeply soulful.

Fans and critics have praised Electric Dusk for its experimental nature and Thomas' smooth, versatile voice. R&B fans, especially those who enjoy artists who push the boundaries of the genre, have found this project to be an exciting addition to the 2023 music landscape. Leon Thomas is carving out a lane of his own and is definitely an artist you should have on your radar.

My Personal Favourite

Crash & Burn

Conclusion

The R&B scene is richer than ever, with artists like Kehlani, Cat Burns, Lucky Daye, Jazmine Sullivan, Victoria Monét, Tems, and Leon Thomas each adding their own flavor to the genre. Whether you’re looking for emotional ballads, modern love anthems, or something a bit more experimental, these artists are keeping R&B alive and thriving. Dive into their latest releases, and you’ll see why they’re essential listening for any fan of the genre.

Top 5 Easiest Dog Breeds to Train for Families

Read Time: 5 minutes

Choosing the right dog breed for your family is an important decision, and ease of training is a major factor to consider - especially if you’re a first-time dog owner. A well-trained dog not only makes life easier but also strengthens the bond between your family and your furry companion. While all dogs can be trained with patience and consistency, some breeds are naturally more receptive to learning commands, routines, and good behavior. If you're looking for a dog that's great with kids and easy to train, here are the top five breeds to consider.

"I love puppies" - Amaria, 2024

1. Labrador Retriever

One of the best family dogs, and arguably one of the easiest breeds to train, is the Labrador Retriever. Known for their friendly demeanor and intelligence, Labradors have a strong desire to bond with their human families, which makes them highly motivated to learn commands and routines. Their eagerness to please, combined with their love of food rewards, makes training an enjoyable process. Labradors are naturally patient and social, which makes them especially great for families with children. Whether you're teaching them basic commands like "sit" and "stay" or advanced tricks, they’re quick to grasp new skills. Their adaptability and willingness to learn make them an ideal choice for active family life.

2. Golden Retriever

Similarly, Golden Retrievers are another breed renowned for their gentle nature and ease of training. Much like Labradors, Golden Retrievers are intelligent, friendly, and eager to please, which allows them to quickly pick up new commands. They thrive on positive reinforcement, making training sessions productive and enjoyable for both the dog and the family. Their calm demeanor and patience also make them excellent companions for children, as they tend to be tolerant and gentle. Golden Retrievers are known for excelling in both basic obedience and more complex tasks, making them a highly versatile breed that fits seamlessly into most family settings.

3. Poodle

Poodles, whether Standard, Miniature, or Toy, are another breed known for their intelligence and trainability. Poodles are quick learners who love mental challenges and enjoy being engaged in training sessions. They are often considered one of the smartest dog breeds, making them an excellent choice for families who want a dog that can quickly pick up new tricks and routines. Poodles are highly obedient when properly trained and tend to be very responsive to positive reinforcement. Their hypoallergenic coats also make them a great option for families with allergy concerns, adding to their appeal as an easy-to-train family dog.

4. Border Collie

For families looking for a breed that combines intelligence with high energy, the Border Collie is an excellent choice. Border Collies are one of the smartest dog breeds in the world, known for their ability to learn commands quickly and excel in agility training. While they do require a lot of physical and mental stimulation due to their high energy levels, Border Collies are eager to learn and thrive in an environment where they have tasks to complete. Their responsiveness to verbal cues and hand signals makes them ideal for families that enjoy outdoor activities or advanced training. For those looking to engage in activities like agility or obedience competitions, the Border Collie is the perfect companion.

5. Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

Lastly, the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel is a breed that combines affection with trainability, making them a wonderful option for families. While they may not have the same energy level as Border Collies or Labradors, Cavaliers are gentle, calm, and eager to please their owners. Their people-oriented nature makes them very responsive to positive reinforcement, and they tend to pick up household rules and commands with ease. Due to their small size and friendly demeanor, they are well-suited for families living in smaller homes or apartments. Cavalier King Charles Spaniels are also great with children, as they are tolerant and enjoy being around people, making them an ideal breed for families looking for a calm and affectionate pet.

Conclusion

Training a dog can be a rewarding experience, especially when you choose a breed that responds well to guidance and commands. Whether you're looking for a high-energy dog like the Border Collie or a more relaxed companion like the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, these five breeds are known for being easy to train and family-friendly. With consistent training, positive reinforcement, and patience, these dogs will quickly become well-behaved and beloved members of your family.

Warm Up This Fall: 7 Cozy Candle Scents from Independent Artisans

Read Time: 5 minutes

As the crisp autumn air settles in and the leaves begin to change, there’s no better way to embrace the season than by filling your home with warm, inviting scents. Candles can instantly transform your space, creating that cozy, comforting atmosphere we all crave in fall. Whether it’s the aroma of spiced pumpkin, warm vanilla, or earthy wood, the right candle can elevate your autumnal experience. Even better, purchasing from independent shops not only supports small businesses but also gives you access to unique, handcrafted creations you won’t find anywhere else. Here are some of the best candle scents to make you feel cozy this autumn, along with links to their independent sellers.

1. Pumpkin Chai - Brooklyn Candle Studio

Few scents capture the essence of fall like the comforting blend of pumpkin and chai spices. Brooklyn Candle Studio’s Pumpkin Chai candle offers a rich, warm aroma that instantly brings autumn into your home. The fragrance combines creamy pumpkin with notes of cinnamon, nutmeg, and clove, with a touch of vanilla for that extra cozy sweetness. Each candle is hand-poured in Brooklyn, made from 100% soy wax for a clean, eco-friendly burn. The minimalist design and natural ingredients make this candle a fall staple.

2. Sweater Weather - Frostbeard Studio

If you’re a book lover, Frostbeard Studio creates candles specifically designed to evoke the cozy feeling of curling up with a good read. Their Sweater Weather candle is the perfect scent to accompany a quiet autumn afternoon. With notes of eucalyptus, mint, and cedar, it has a refreshing yet warm aroma that captures the crispness of the outdoors while bringing a soothing calm indoors. Made with soy wax and phthalate-free fragrance oils, this candle is a great option for anyone looking to enhance their fall reading nook.

3. Autumn Leaves - P.F. Candle Co.

For those who love the earthy, natural scents of autumn, P.F. Candle Co.’s Autumn Leaves candle is a must. This hand-poured soy wax candle blends the fresh, crisp scent of fallen leaves with cedar and pine, transporting you to a walk through a forest on a brisk autumn day. It’s the perfect scent to bring the outdoors inside, especially if you love nature-inspired fragrances. P.F. Candle Co. is known for its sustainable practices, using recyclable packaging and domestically grown soy wax, making this a feel-good purchase in more ways than one.

4. Vanilla Bourbon - Burke & Hare Co.

For those who prefer something a little richer and more indulgent, Burke & Hare Co. offers the luxurious Vanilla Bourbon candle. This scent blends the warm, creamy aroma of vanilla with deep bourbon undertones, making it perfect for cozy autumn evenings by the fire. The richness of the bourbon adds a layer of sophistication, while the vanilla keeps it grounded in comfort. Hand-poured in small batches, this candle also features a unique black wax, making it a beautiful addition to your fall decor.

5. Woodland Walk - Wax & Wool

If you want a candle that evokes the feeling of taking a peaceful walk through an autumn forest, Wax & Wool’s Woodland Walk is a perfect choice. This candle combines notes of fir, cedarwood, and earth for a deep, grounding scent that brings the essence of the woods into your home. It’s the ideal candle for those who love nature and want to feel connected to the changing season even when indoors. Wax & Wool candles are made from pure soy wax and are hand-poured in small batches, ensuring high-quality craftsmanship.

6. Spiced Apple Cider - Cozy Blue

What could be more quintessentially autumn than the smell of warm apple cider simmering with cinnamon and cloves? Cozy Blue’s Spiced Apple Cider candle delivers that exact experience in a jar. This small-batch candle is handcrafted with a soy wax blend and infused with natural fragrance oils that perfectly capture the essence of a cozy fall day spent sipping cider by the fireplace. Its sweet, spicy scent fills the room with warmth and comfort, making it ideal for both everyday burning and special seasonal moments.

7. Fireside - Keap Candles

For those who crave the smoky, nostalgic scent of a wood-burning fire, Keap Candles’ Fireside candle is the ultimate autumn companion. This candle has an inviting, campfire-like aroma, with notes of vetiver, smoke, and cedarwood that instantly bring warmth and coziness to any space. Keap Candles are made with sustainable, coconut-based wax and use lead-free cotton wicks, ensuring a clean, long-lasting burn. The minimalist, eco-friendly design and clean ingredients make Fireside a great choice for environmentally conscious candle lovers.
